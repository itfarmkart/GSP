Born on 16th February 1975 in Barwani, Madhya Pradesh, Gajendra Singh Patel comes from a family where service, education, and social responsibility were core values. His father, the late Shri Umrao Singh Patel, was a former Education Minister of Madhya Pradesh.

With a background in BA and LLB, his education instilled in him a deep understanding of constitutional values and social justice. For over 34 years, he has been tirelessly working for the organization and society.
